CLASSIFIED // THE CUPID VIRUS

THE MEET-CUTE (THE BEGINNING)

It started under the neon lights of the Tech Valley Gala. S.M.A.S. Intelligence Chief Blue Monroe thought she was having a drink with a charismatic Silicon Valley billionaire. She didn't know Julian Mustard was the heir to a ruthless crime syndicate, and she definitely didn't know his "Stealth Wealth" charm was mathematically engineered.

THE INFECTION (THE MIDDLE)

Julian isn't just typing on a keyboard; he *is* the hardware. Through synthetic subdermal pheromones and optic-nerve hacking, he infected Blue with the "Cupid Virus." Her psychological firewalls dropped. As they fell deeper in love, Julian quietly gained backdoor access to the S.M.A.S. mainframes. He used her heart to hack her agency.

THE COUNTDOWN (ALMOST THE END)

Blue is compromised, completely oblivious to the trap snapping shut around her. Now, the entire S.M.A.S. network is exposed to Syncor. The only thing standing in Julian's way is Special Operative Imani Sinclair, who is racing against the clock to break the biological conditioning before Blue hands over the rebellion's ultimate secret.
