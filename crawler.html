SPICY REBELLION
ASSET WAR

A new era of power has begun.

In a world where governments, billionaires, and secret syndicates compete for control of the future, one elite organization operates in plain sight...

S.M.A.S.

Strategic Military Asset Solutions.

Feared. Respected. Untouchable. They retrieve what others cannot. They recover what nations lose. They protect what the world cannot afford to see destroyed.

At the center of this empire stand three women whose alliance has reshaped global power.

Shannon “Too Hot”
A strategist with unmatched intuition and dangerous charm.

Imani Sinclair
Cybersecurity genius and silent architect of digital warfare.

And Blue Monroe…
The most beautiful weapon ever created. A synthetic human with evolving consciousness and unknown origins.

Together they built wealth, influence, and a network that reaches every continent. But S.M.A.S. is not their only operation.

Behind the scenes, a shell company known as SYNCOR develops advanced technologies, experimental weapons, and life-saving scientific breakthroughs for governments and private elites.

And hidden even deeper… A secret recipe.

A legendary hot sauce formula tied to power, loyalty, and global underground networks. Its guardian is Imani’s cousin, a mysterious humanitarian known only as the Bootlegging Queen. A street legend who has never been caught.

Now, new enemies are emerging. A brilliant and dangerous scientist has begun searching for the truth behind Blue Monroe.

And if he succeeds… The balance of power will collapse.

The war for the future has already begun.
