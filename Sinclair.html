THE SINCLAIR SISTERS

While Blue Monroe handles the intelligence, the Sinclair family handles the survival of the collective. Special Operative Imani Sinclair is the tactical blade of S.M.A.S., currently fighting to save Blue from Julian's trap. But the true heart of the rebellion belongs to her sister, Shannon "Too Hot" Sinclair.

THE SECRET RECIPE

How does an underground dissident network afford high-tech armor, encrypted servers, and a fleet of Tactical Taco Trucks? It all comes back to a single bottle. For decades, Shannon "Too Hot" Sinclair has been running a full-blown, classified production facility out of the main S.M.A.S. safe house.

THE SPICY REBELLION

The product is the Spicy Rebellion Hot Sauce. It is armed with a highly guarded secret recipe that delivers a coughing-hot strike to the palate, followed by an agonizingly delicious aftertaste. The demand is relentless. The return customers and continuous requests fund the entire war against Syncor. The Mustard Dynasty wants to steal the formula; S.M.A.S. will burn the city down to protect it.
